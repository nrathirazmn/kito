# Vision

## Vision Statement

Become the single source of truth for group coordination.

---

## Problem Statement

Planning a group trip today often requires juggling multiple applications. Conversations happen in WhatsApp, payments are tracked elsewhere, ideas are scattered across TikTok and Instagram, links are buried in chats, and plans are written in shared documents. As information becomes fragmented, groups spend more time searching than coordinating.

Kito exists to eliminate this friction by providing one trusted place where every important piece of trip information lives.

---

## Mission

Reduce the chaos of group coordination by making important information easy to organize, find, and share.

---

## Product Vision

Kito is a collaborative workspace built for group trips.

Rather than replacing communication platforms like WhatsApp, Kito complements them by becoming the group's single source of truth. It centralizes important information—including payments, shared finds, checklists, plans, and useful links—into one structured workspace that everyone can rely on.

---

## Product Principles

### 1. Single Source of Truth

Every important piece of information should have one canonical home.

---

### 2. Collaboration First

Every feature should make group coordination easier.

---

### 3. Simplicity Wins

Prefer fewer well-designed features over many average ones.

---

### 4. Reduce Cognitive Load

Users should spend less time searching for information and more time enjoying their trip.

---

### 5. Mobile First

Design for phones before desktops.

---

### 6. Purpose Before Features

Every feature must solve a real coordination problem.

---

## Target Audience

### Primary

- Friends planning trips together

### Secondary

- University clubs
- Student organizations
- Small travel groups

### Future

- Corporate retreats
- Volunteer programmes
- Team offsites

---

## Non Goals

Kito is not:

- A booking platform
- A messaging application
- A travel agency
- A map application
- A replacement for WhatsApp
- A social media platform
- An AI itinerary planner

---

## Success Metrics

Kito succeeds when:

- Groups instinctively open Kito whenever they need trip information.
- No important information is lost throughout the trip.
- Groups spend significantly less time searching through chats.
- Every member knows exactly where to find the latest information.
