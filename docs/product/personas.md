# Personas

## Persona 1 — The Planner

### Description

Usually the person who creates the itinerary, books accommodations, and keeps everyone organized.

### Goals

- Keep everyone updated
- Reduce repeated questions
- Track payments
- Store important links

### Pain Points

- Information scattered across apps
- People asking the same questions repeatedly
- Difficulty tracking who has paid

---

## Persona 2 — The Contributor

### Description

Actively sends TikToks, restaurant recommendations, and attractions but rarely organizes them.

### Goals

- Easily save trip ideas
- Share discoveries
- View updated plans

### Pain Points

- Losing TikTok links
- Forgetting recommendations
- Difficulty finding previously shared content

---

## Persona 3 — The Casual Member

### Description

Joins the trip but doesn't contribute much to planning.

### Goals

- Quickly check important information
- Know what needs attention
- View itinerary

### Pain Points

- Doesn't know where information is stored
- Constantly asks where links or plans are
